# 4.1.2 | Critical Thinking Questions

Answer the following critical thinking questions. In your answers, be sure to use specific examples from the assigned text to support your answer. Refer to specific people or specific events or moments. Name names. Use proper nouns. Don't forget to include a citation to a page number where you found the example. This should be done for each question.

1. Explain the difference between the establishment clause and the free exercise clause, and explain how these two clauses work together to guarantee religious freedoms.
2. What are some of the ways that free speech/free expression is restricted in the United States, in other words, what are some of the limitations of the First Amendment?
3. What are some of the ways that gun ownership is restricted in the United States, in other words, what are some of the limitations of the Second Amendment?
4. Describe two different ways that the 4th Amendment protects U.S. citizens.
5. What makes the "right to privacy" a unique right? How has this right been interpreted by the U.S. Supreme Court?
