# 6.2 | Critical Thinking Questions

Answer the following critical thinking questions. In your answers, be sure to use specific examples from the assigned text to support your answer. Refer to specific people or specific events or moments. Name names. Use proper nouns. Don't forget to include a citation to a page number where you found the example. This should be done for each question.

1. Explain how three different agents of socialization can impact an individual's political opinions.
2. What is the difference between liberal and conservative? Left wing vs. right wing?
3. Describe two important standards that pollsters should follow when attempting to conduct public opinion surveys.
4. Describe two problems or challenges that pollsters run into when attempting to conduct public opinion surveys.
5. Why would House of Representative members be more interested in public opinion than Senators, Presidents or members of the Supreme Court?
