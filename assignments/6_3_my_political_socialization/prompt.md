# 6.3 | "My Political Socialization"

Briefly describe yourself and your Political Socialization. What made you the political person, with your own unique political views, that you have become? What event/events have shaped you politically? Which individuals have had the biggest impact on your political opinions? Do you consider yourself to be liberal, conservative, libertarian, or something else? How did that develop?

**Writing tips:** Add any relevant information that will help the reader better understand the way you have developed opinions on political issues. Be specific. Refer to specific issues and specific events or moments. Name major events. Use proper nouns. Points will be deducted for vagueness or overly broad generalizations.

This submission should be 500 words minimum. Submissions that are under the word count will only receive a fraction of the total points (ex. a 100 word submission will receive a maximum grade of 20%).
