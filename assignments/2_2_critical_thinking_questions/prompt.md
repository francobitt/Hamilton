# 2.2 | Critical Thinking Questions

Answer the following critical thinking questions. In your answers, be sure to use specific examples from the assigned text to support your answer. Refer to specific people or specific events or moments. Name names. Use proper nouns. Don't forget to include a citation to a page number where you found the example. This should be done for each question.

1. What political ideals were influential in the decision to declare independence from Britain?
2. What were the flaws or weaknesses of the Articles of Confederation?
3. Explain two compromises that were made during the Constitutional convention.
4. Why did some Americans feel that the Bill of Rights was a necessary addition to the Constitution? Why did some Americans feel that it was unnecessary?
5. Of the amendments that have been added to the U.S. Constitution, why might the 14th Amendment be the most important addition?
