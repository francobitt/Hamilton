# 7.2 | Critical Thinking Questions

Answer the following critical thinking questions. In your answers, be sure to use specific examples from the assigned text to support your answer. Refer to specific people or specific events or moments. Name names. Use proper nouns. Don't forget to include a citation to a page number where you found the example. This should be done for each question.

1. How have some states attempted to use voter registration laws to make it harder for people to vote?
2. Explain two factors that can decrease voter turnout in the United States.
3. What is the difference between a political primary and a political convention? What role do each play in the electoral process?
4. How does social media affect elections and campaigns? Explain one positive impact and one negative impact that social media has had on elections and campaigns.
5. Describe one positive consequence and one negative consequence of ballot initiatives.
