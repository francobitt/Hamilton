# 9.3 | Project: Who Do I Side With?

You are required to complete this assignment on a Google Doc. When you turn in your assignment, copy-paste your answers into the answer box, provide the link, and give your teacher editing privileges to your document. You must submit both.

Have you ever wondered where you fall on the political spectrum? Have you ever found yourself wondering if there are more than two political parties out there? Here is a chance to start exploring.

**Step 1:** You will begin this assignment by taking the "I Side With" quiz.

**Step 2:** Once you have your results take a screenshot of the results. Your screenshot must include the web link on top and the time and date in the corner. Insert the image to either the beginning of your reflection or the end. (25 points)

**Step 3:** Write a 500 word (minimum) reflection to your results (50 points). Your reflection must include the following:
- Which party or ideology did you most closely align with? Were you surprised by the results? Why or why not? Are there any specific issues you think influenced your results? Do you think your results accurately depict you and your personal beliefs? (Why or why not?)
- Choose one topic from within the quiz and write about it from an opposing viewpoint. Choose a major issue that was presented in the quiz (such as immigration, education, gun control, etc.)
- State your position on the issue and why you hold this view. Use evidence, personal experience, or reasoning to support your view.
- Consider the viewpoint that is opposite of your own. Explain this viewpoint accurately and fairly. Describe why people may hold this view using reliable sources, personal experience, or reasoning. Discuss any points in the opposing argument that you can understand or agree with.
- Finish with a conclusion that explains whether this experience helped you to understand not only your own views, but the viewpoints of others.
