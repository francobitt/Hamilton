# 3.2 | Critical Thinking Questions

Answer the following critical thinking questions. In your answers, be sure to use specific examples from the assigned text to support your answer. Refer to specific people or specific events or moments. Name names. Use proper nouns. Don't forget to include a citation to a page number where you found the example. This should be done for each question.

1. What unique powers does the federal government exercise?
2. What unique powers do state governments exercise?
3. What unique powers do local governments exercise?
4. How have the relationships between the states and federal government evolved since the early 1800s?
5. What are some of the various ways that the federal government can shape the actions of state and local governments?
